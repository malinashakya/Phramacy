About Us

PharmaWeb is an online application, that contains information about thousands of drugs available in Nepal.It is a must have application for the health professionals all over Nepal. This application makes new pharmacist easier to know what brands medicinesare available and their products as well. This page also tells who are stockist inside Kathmandu valley and also provides the contact information about the stockists, so that they can directly send email to order their required products.
